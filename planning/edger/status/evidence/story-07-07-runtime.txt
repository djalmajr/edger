```text

edger-ext-auth: 6 passed
edger-ext-gateway/tests/gateway_middleware.rs: 22 passed; 1 failed
```

The single failure is the known sandbox loopback bind failure in
`edger-ext-gateway/tests/gateway_middleware.rs`:

```text
proxy_rule_forwards_to_local_upstream_without_sensitive_headers
called `Result::unwrap()` on an `Err` value:
Os { code: 1, kind: PermissionDenied, message: "Operation not permitted" }
```

The failing test binds `127.0.0.1:0`; the sandbox denies that bind. The test was
left unchanged.

  rate without depending on Deno or network.
